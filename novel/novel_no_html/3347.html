Buy Lead Oxide (Battery Oxide) Mixture

This is an inquiry message about Buy Lead Oxide (Battery Oxide) Mixture from the 109th China Canton Fair.

(这是一条关于"Buy Lead Oxide (Battery Oxide) Mixture"的询盘信息，来自第109届中国广交会。)

Inquiry Message (询盘信息)
Purchase Product (采购产品): 
Buy Lead Oxide (Battery Oxide) Mixture
Detail Content (询盘内容): 
Detailed Buying Lead Description We are in need of the following item; Technical Specs. 1-Purity 99, 99% 2-PbO level in weight between 67% and 70% (will be tested based on TS 6616) 3-Free lead ratio in weight between 30% and 33% 4-Acid absorbation capacity (gH2SO4) between 15% and 25% per 100 gram 5-Size of the lead piece 280micronm staying on the sieve which is 0, 5% max (rest will drop on the ground) 6-Ratio of silver, arsenic, copper, iron, antimon, tin and zinc in the mixture at 10ppm max 7-Ratio of bizmut in the mixture at 50ppm max 8-No humidity in material mixture (will be tested based on TS 2899) 9-Packing: 50kg-air free Polyethylene bags, then Jute bags (doubled inner bags) - 20pcsX50kg in a wooden box PS. Specs 6 and 7 above (will be tested based on atomic absorbation, spectrometer, ICP, MS or wet methods) Please get back to us with your quoation by June 1, 07 latest along with payment terms, delivery lead time.
