Exploring rationales and effects of quantitative expansion of school organizations is so significant practically for the adjustment and optimization on organizational structure. This research is to investigate the process of expanding in the number of schools in HUST by interviewing the relevant personnel and reviewing the historical archives. The purpose of this research is to examine rationales and effects of quantitative expansion of school organizations based upon describing the process of organizational change. Causes for quantitative expansion varies a lot. In national level, special policies in each time of China (such as the adjustment of national higher education in 1952, enrollment expansion from 1999, the merging of universities around 2000) and ingrained "official culture" are much related with this expansion; in institutional level, the requirements of implementing comprehensive strategy, establishing world-class university and strengthening institutional powers accelerated organizational differentiation and the adding of new schools or departments; as long as schools or departments, the new are built by pursuing organizational or individual interests. These causes in three levels are in their interaction. Most interviewees considered that the number of schools cannot affect the university because university is a "loosely connected" organization with uniqueness of each part; Some interviewees believed that organizational differentiation strongly contributed to relieve contradictions, maintain stability, and promote development; the smallest part of interviewees worried about such negativeness of schools or departments' redundance as the weakening of institutional power, the increasing of administrative costs, and the reducing of administrative efficiency. So far, the adjustment of schools and departments involves with many difficulties. The impedance comes from the interest group represented by deans in each school or II 华 中 科 技 大 学 硕 士 学 位 论 文 department. Change is a key path for organizational development and its success are determined by the competition of each interest groups. Thus, in order to reduce the resistance from different interest groups, the efforts of analyzing demands from every interest group and respecting history and concerns on all sides should be taken by people who devote to the adjustment. 诞生于清朝末年，此后由于战火连年，大学生存雪上加霜。新中国成立后，环境虽 然较为稳定，但因特殊的国际国内局势，许多大学在 1952 年院系调整后变得“面 目全非”，却也有一部分大学因此应运而生，成为新中国高等教育史上的奇葩，其 中，被誉为“江城明珠”的华中科技大学便是一例。华中科技大学是 2000 年 5 月 26 日由同济医科大学、武汉城市建设学院与华中理工大学合并而来的。其中，原华 中理工大学由原华中工学院演变而来，她被称为“新中国高等教育发展的缩影”。 为了更加深入地了解我国高等院校的院系组织的历史变迁，笔者以华中科技大学为 案例进行了剖析，按照原华中工学院——原华中理工大学——华中科技大学的线索 详细研究了内部院系组织的演化历程。 图 1.1.1-1 华中科技大学自建校以来的院系组织数量的变化 3 华 中 科 技 大 学 硕 士 学 位 论 文 （资料来源：2011 个院系；改革开放后，该校继往开来，至 1988 年 1 月更名为华中理工大学之时，已经拥有了 20 多个院系，成为了涵盖文、理、工、 管等多种学科门类的多科型大学；步入 90 年代后，学校更是抓住机遇，不断发展， 至 2000 年 5 月合并为华中科技大学时，学校院系组织数量已经为 40 个，成为一所 图 涵盖十一大学科门类的综合型大学。 1.1.1-1 已经很形象地显示了华中科技大学近 60 推动还是大学发展的内在逻辑？纵观各类文件及各项研究，几乎没有一项文本对此 现象产生的原因进行深入剖析，理清“来龙”方能明了“去脉”，探明其生成原因 才能更好地把握大学的未来之路。在建设高等教育强国的背景下，院系组织数量是 否该无限制地扩张？数量的多寡对高校的运行到底有无影响？如果有，又是何影 响？是积极的还是消极的？我国大学的未来又该如何发展，内部组织结构又该作何 调整？如果调整，在调整过程中会遇到什么阻力，谁会支持？谁会反对？最终改革 又将如何进行？一系列的问号打开了求知的渴望，于是带着这份渴望，笔者便 踏上了这条漫漫求索之路。 1.1.2 研究意义 本研究并不是空洞的直述，也不是乏味的理论，而是理论与现实的双重结合。 可以说，本选题具有一定的理论意义和现实意义。 4 华 中 科 技 大 学 硕 士 学 位 论 文 、
